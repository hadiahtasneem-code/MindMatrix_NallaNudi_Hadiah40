# Nalla-Nudi 🌱📚

## Overview
Nalla-Nudi is an offline Android application developed to help Kannada-medium students transition smoothly into English-medium higher education. Many rural students understand concepts clearly in Kannada but struggle with English technical vocabulary used in subjects like Science, Mathematics, Commerce, and Computer Science.

The app acts as a bridge-dictionary for technical terms by providing:
- English technical words
- Simple Kannada meanings and explanations
- Pronunciation guidance
- Subject-wise learning support

Nalla-Nudi is designed as a confidence-building educational tool that removes language barriers and supports inclusive learning.

---

# Problem Statement
Students from Kannada-medium schools often face challenges while transitioning to English for higher education. Although they understand concepts well, they struggle with technical vocabulary such as:
- Photosynthesis
- Trigonometry
- Algorithm
- Economics terms

Traditional dictionaries are too broad and do not provide subject-specific contextual meanings that students can easily understand.

---

# Vision
Nalla-Nudi serves as a “Bridge Dictionary for Technical Terms” focused on helping students during their educational transition phase.

The application enables students to:
- Search technical terms instantly
- Learn simple Kannada explanations
- Hear correct English pronunciation
- Build confidence in technical communication

The project promotes both educational equality and linguistic pride by using the mother tongue as a ladder to learn global languages.

---

# Features ✨

## 🔍 Instant Term Search
Search English technical words and get:
- Kannada meaning
- Contextual explanation
- Simple examples

Example:
Gravity → Gurutvakarshane + Explanation

---

## 📚 Subject Filters
Students can browse vocabulary based on subjects:
- Science
- Mathematics
- Commerce
- Biology
- Physics
- Computer Science

---

## ⭐ My List
Users can save difficult words for:
- Daily revision
- Quick review
- Flashcard-style learning

---

## 🔊 Voice Pronunciation Guide
Tap the speaker icon to hear the correct pronunciation of English technical terms using Android Text-To-Speech.

---

## 📴 Fully Offline Support
The application works completely offline without requiring internet access.
